## Sales Data Dashboard (Power BI)

### Business Problem
The goal of this project was to analyze overall sales performance to identify trends in revenue, transaction volume, and product movement, and to uncover factors influencing business growth or decline.

### Approach
- Cleaned and prepared raw sales data for analysis  
- Built a data model to track performance across products and time periods  
- Developed DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as total sales, average transaction value, and product performance  
- Designed an interactive dashboard for real-time monitoring of business metrics  

### Key Insights
- A small group of products contributed significantly to total revenue, indicating dependency on top performers  
- Transaction volume and revenue trends revealed fluctuations across different time periods  
- Certain products showed consistently low performance, highlighting opportunities for optimization or removal  
- Average transaction value trends helped identify customer purchasing behavior  

### Impact
This dashboard can help businesses:
- Monitor sales performance in real time  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming products  
- Optimize inventory and product strategy  
- Support data-driven decision-making for revenue growth  

### Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard Development, DAX, Data Modeling)  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  

### Features
- KPI tracking (Sales, Transactions, Average Value)  
- Interactive filters for product and time analysis  
- Visual breakdown of sales trends and product performance
